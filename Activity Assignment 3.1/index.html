A Resort Hotel is a full-service lodging facility, intended primarily for vacationers and usually located in places frequented for relaxation or recreation, such as beaches, seashores, scenic or historic areas, ski parks, spas. Deluxe Rooms

Suite

A suite is a much larger accommodation. It usually has an attached bathroom, a living area, and most times, includes a dining area as well.
